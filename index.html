Katherine Anne Cope

Throughout my life, I have always had a sincere passion for three things: music, animals, and helping others. During my time at the University of Georgia, I have allowed myself to explore all three.
I regularly perform at the Hugh Hodgson School of Music, I am a foster and volunteer at the Athens Area Humane Society, and I am a Communication Studies major with a minor in French.

While also studying at the University of Georgia, I have involved myself in numerous clubs. I am a member of the Redcoat Marching Band and I am in the Drumline. I am also a member of Sigma Alpha Iota, a music Fraternity for women.

"The University of Georgia is located in the music-rich city of Athens, Georgia. Sigma Alpha Iota (SAI) is an international music fraternity. Music is our main focus; whether it is in:

  • composition
  • conducting
  • performance
  • appreciation

SAI has it all! SAI harbors a strong interest in our members' abilities to succeed in their musical endeavors, career development, and in giving back to their communities. The Iota Zeta chapter at UGA is in Region VI and Province Tau A."

I pride myself in my ability to attempt new adventures and be courageous in my decisions. Even if that means jumping out of a plane or performing in a stadium filled with over 90,000 people.
